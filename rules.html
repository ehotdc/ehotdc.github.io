RULES

Yes, you of course have to abide by rules in this CTF. TL;DR: be nice, don't not be nice.

  1. You will need to participate in teams of 2-5 people.
  2. The event requires physical attendance for the majority of the team. (2 person teams need both onsite, 3 person teams need 2 onsite, 4 person teams need 3 onsite, 5 person teams need 3 onsite).
  3. To attend physically you are required to provide national identification on the day, passport or drivers license.
  4. All on-site participants are required to secure a ticket for the event, this is free and done through eventbrite. Physical attendance requires a ticket.
  5. You may only participate if you are a Danish resident (Living in Denmark) or a Danish citizen living abroad.
  6. Teams are prohibited from sharing flags with other teams. Please just don't do it.
  7. Do not attack the competition's infrastructure, don't attack CTFd etc.
  8. Don't attack other teams challenge instances.
  9. Unless specified, do not attempt to perform container/vm escapes.
  10. If you discover a mistake in a challenge or the infrastructure, please contact us.
  11. The winning team is determined by the highest points scored at the end of the competition. If more than 1 team is at the top of the scoreboard, then the time of scoring is used to determine the winner.
  12. When at the venue, please do not leave the designated areas. We perform our own physical penetration tests, we don't need outside help :-) .

Breaking the rules means exclusion from the event.
