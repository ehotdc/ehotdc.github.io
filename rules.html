RULES

Yes, you of course have to abide by rules in this CTF. TL;DR: be nice, don't not be nice.

  1. You will need to participate in teams. 3-4 persons.
  2. The event requires physical attendance for the majority of the team. (3 person teams may have 1 remote player, 4 person teams may have 2).
  3. All on-site participants are required to secure a ticket for the event, this is free and done through cyberskills and eventbrite.
  4. Teams are prohibited from sharing flags with other teams. Please just don't do it.
  5. Do not attack the competition's infrastructure, don't attack CTFd etc.
  6. Don't attack other teams challenge instances.
  7. Unless specified, do not attempt to perform container/vm escapes.
  8. If you discover a mistake in a challenge or the infrastructure, please contact us.
  9. The winning team is determined by the highest points scored at the end of the competition. If more than 1 team is at the top of the scoreboard, then the time of scoring is used to determine the winner.
  10. When at the venue, please do not leave the designated areas. We perform our own physical penetration tests, we don't need outside help :-) .
