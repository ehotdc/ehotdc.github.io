FAQ

Q1: How many members can a team have?
A: A team can have 3 or 4 members.

Q2: Is the event physical or remote
A: The event will be mainly physical, a team may have up to half of their members sitting remote (1 person for 3 person teams, 2 persons for 4 person teams).

Q3: How do I register for the competition?
A: You can register through eventbrite.

Q4: Will there be prizes??
A: Yes, there will be prizes for the top 3 teams. They are to be announced.

Q5: How do I register for the competition?
A: You can register through the event's official website.

Q6: What if I don't have a team?
A: Sign up and show up, we will make sure everyone has a team before we start.

Q7: I pwned the challenge, where can I find the flag?
A: In the following locations / , /root , C:/. Unless otherwise specified.

Q8: What special hardware will I need to bring?
A: Just your computer! If you will need some special equipment, we will provide it.

Q9: What is the flag format?
A: The flag format will be:

Q10: What about food and drinks?
A: We provide all food and drinks. We will of course try to cater to as many preferences as possible, non-pork, vegan, pesca-pescatarians and alike, but if we appreciate a heads up if you have a unique dietary restriction :-)

Q11: Will there be talks and workshops?
A: This year we wanted to have more time for the CTF, so we will only give an introductory talk and welcome, so you can get straight to hacking.
